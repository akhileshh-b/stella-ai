About Stella AI

Stella AI is an AI-powered emotional support assistant designed to help individuals experiencing anxiety, depression, or emotional distress. By combining advanced natural language processing and sentiment analysis, Stella provides real-time, empathetic responses to users in need.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between technology and emotional well-being, offering a safe and supportive space for users to express their feelings.
